Development and evaluation of a computationally inexpensive model to simulate the occupational exposure to VOCs

Applications of our software are:

A robust tool to support drug/chemical safety assessment agencies. This include characterization of the behavior of the chemical within the body.
This can also be adopted and customized by public health. With known emissions our model can predict values of actual exposure concentrations which is another important step in chemical risk assessment.
Cosmetics suppliers who would like to place cosmetic products on the market have to comply with the regulations like Cosmetics Directive.
Assessment of the safety for human health of the finished product can be done by our model by knowing the chemicals used in that certain product.
